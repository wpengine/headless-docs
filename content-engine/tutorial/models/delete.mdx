## Delete a Model

A model is a container that holds fields for use in content generation in publisher.

### Steps to Delete a Model
---
1. Click on Content Modeler in the WordPress side menu

    ![Content Engine sync](../../images/acm-sidebar.png)

1. If it's not on Models, click Models under Content Modeler in the WordPress side menu

1. You will see the current models listed. Click the triple dot menu and select Delete from the popup menu

    ![Content Engine sync](../../images/delete-model.png)

1. Confirm deletion by clicking Delete in the modal that pops up. Click Cancel to keep the model
    
    ![Content Engine sync](../../images/Screen_Shot_2021-09-01_at_12.08.32_PM.png)
